Biriyani

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful mixed rice dish with roots in the Indian subcontinent. It is prepared by layering long-grain basmati rice with marinated meat, fish, or vegetables, along with an aromatic blend of spices.

Fish Meals

Fish meals are a valuable source of protein and essential fatty acids, making them a cornerstone of many balanced diets. They are rich in Omega-3 fatty acids, which are crucial for brain health and reducing inflammation. From simple grilled fillets to elaborate seafood dishes, fish meals offer a wide variety of flavors and textures.

Paratha

Paratha with fish gravy is a delightful combination, bringing together the flaky, layered texture of the Indian flatbread with the rich and flavorful sauce. The paratha, whether plain or stuffed, provides a perfect vehicle to soak up the aromatic spices and tender fish from the gravy.

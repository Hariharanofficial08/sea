Our Story


Welcome to Searestaurant, where the ocean meets your plate. Founded in 2002 by Julite , our restaurant was born from a passion for fresh, high-quality seafood and a desire to create a memorable dining experience. We believe that great food starts with great ingredients, which is why we partner with local fishermen to bring you the best catch of the day.

Our culinary team, led by head chef Thaamu, crafts each dish with a perfect blend of traditional techniques and modern flavors. From our signature grilled lobster to our classic fish and chips, every item on our menu tells a story of the sea. We are dedicated to sustainability and ensuring that we serve our community and the environment responsibly.
